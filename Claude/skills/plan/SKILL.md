# Planning

Require an approved `SPEC.md` for non-trivial work. Analyze read-only, identify dependencies, and split work into vertical slices. Every task must include scope, acceptance criteria, validation, and rollback notes where relevant.

Write `tasks/plan.md` and `tasks/todo.md`. Do not modify business code. Surface unresolved decisions instead of hiding them.

Use `references/workflows/planning-and-task-breakdown/`, `context-engineering/`, and `doubt-driven-development/` when deeper planning guidance is required.
